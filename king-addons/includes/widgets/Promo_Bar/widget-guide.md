# Announcement / Promo Bar

Sticky top/bottom bar for announcements, promos, or notices with CTA and close button.

## Free features
- Position top or bottom; sticky on scroll.
- Badge, title, description, CTA button, close button.
- Styling: background, border, shadow; text/badge/button typography and colors; padding.
- Close hides the bar (localStorage) with simple conditions placeholder.

## Pro features
- Display conditions: page/path, UTM parameters, schedule (start/end).
- Persistent close with expiration, device targeting.
- Countdown timer, button icon, animations, bottom-sheet style.
- Extra spacing/offset controls and advanced styling.

## How to use
1. Add **Announcement / Promo Bar**.
2. Fill badge/title/description and CTA text/link.
3. Choose position (top/bottom) and sticky on scroll.
4. Style background/border/shadow, badge/text/button.
5. (Pro) Set conditions (paths, UTM), schedule, device targeting, countdown, and close persistence.
